How does MintingWorks works?

1. Tell us about yourself and your financial goals.

We ask you a set of questions to understand your financial needs better. Your financial history and goals are used to create an investment plan tailored to your needs.

2. Our platform recommends an optimized plan to help you achieve the goal.

We look through hundreds of financial products and select the ones best suited for achievement of your goals. Investments are made post your acceptance of the plan.

3. Sit back and relax, we'll monitor progress of your financial goals.

Our platform evaluates progress towards your goals 24x7 and recommends course corrections if required. Systematic changes are made based on term remaining for investments. Tactical changes are made to keep up with fluctuations in market conditions. Redeem/reinvest your investments as and when you achieve your goals.
