About us

At MintingWorks we have only one goal - helping you realize you financial goals. We truly believe your money deserves the best treatment. With MintingWorks we want to make personal finance management simple and intuitive.
Most of us seek help with financial matters in our circle of friends or family. But financial planning is an ongoing exercise and not a one-time effort. The vastness of the financial sector with a multitude of products along with its ever changing nature makes the task even more complex. At MintingWorks, we build solutions that will customize the choice of financial products based on your specific needs. With daily monitoring we help you stay on course to achieve your goals.
