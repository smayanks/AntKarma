About us

At Antkarma we have only one goal - helping you to save your hard earned money. We truly believe your money deserves the best treatment. We levarage cutting edge technology along with the sound advise of our financial advisors to build the right investment portfolio for you.
We realized that a lot of people feel wealth management is complex, obsure and time consuming affair. With Antkarma we are going to change that. We aim to bring transparency in wealth management system by building simple, easy to use financial management application.
We envision everyone who invests through Antkarma should feel confident about their financial future. We want to help our technology saavy investors in realizing their ambitions and dreams without worrying about finances.
